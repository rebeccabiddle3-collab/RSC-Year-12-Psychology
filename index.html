Checkpoint 1 📅 Week 6 · Lessons 4–6

Proposal Form — one per group, submitted via QLearn

Before you can run the experiment, your group's proposal must be approved by Mrs Biddle. This includes your experiment choice, research question, ethics considerations, and methodology plan.

✅ "Pracs to Choose From" worksheet uploaded to QLearn ✅ Proposal form completed (one per group) ✅ Ethics forms included ✅ Experiment organised and ready to run ⚠️ Group task

Checkpoint 2 📅 Week 7 · Lessons 7–11

Viewing & Discussion Only — Rationale, RQ, Modifications, Ethics

Mrs Biddle will view and discuss your progress — no formal drafting required. Show what you have written so far for the first sections of your report.

✅ Rationale drafted ✅ Research question written ✅ Modifications described ✅ Safety & ethical considerations included ✅ Experiment run, data collected ✅ Data processing underway
3
Checkpoint 3 📅 Week 8 · Lessons 12–13

Viewing & Discussion Only — Data Analysis & Evidence Evaluation

Again, no formal draft — Mrs Biddle views your analysis work in progress. Your data section and limitations/reliability section should be mostly written.

✅ Symmetry of data determined (histogram checked) ✅ Mean and SD justified — no outliers present ✅ Descriptive statistics calculated ✅ Statistical test run (t-test) ✅ Graph with error bars constructed ✅ Analysis of evidence paragraph drafted ✅ Limitations & reliability section underway
4
Checkpoint 4 ⚠️ Draft Due 📅 Week 9 · Lessons 14–16

Full Draft Submitted — Content in EVERY Section

This is your formal draft submission. Mrs Biddle will annotate up to 2000 words. Any content beyond 2000 words receives only one generalised comment. Every section of the report must have content.

✅ All sections written ✅ ≤ 2000 words ✅ Submitted via QLearn + Turnitin ✅ Visual Journal submitted with draft ⚠️ Individual task from here

Checkpoint 5 📅 Term 2 Week 1

Feedback Returned — Revise Your Report

Drafts are returned with teacher feedback and class feedback given. Read all comments carefully and make targeted revisions before final submission.

✅ Read all teacher comments ✅ Reflect in Visual Journal ✅ Revise report

Checkpoint 6 — Final Submission 📅 Term 2 Week 2 · 30 April 2026 · 11:59pm

Submit via QLearn and Turnitin

Submit your final report electronically by 11:59pm on 30 April 2026. Also hand in hard copies of your final report and Visual Journal to Mrs Biddle.
